FAQ

Have Questions?

What is Coffee?

Coffeetheapp is a mobile app that aims to humanize the job & internship search process by allowing millennials to casually grow their network by connecting with real startup founders, hiring managers & other entry level employees.

How does Coffee work?

The user signs in with their Facebook and/or Linkedin account. Then you are sent through a setup process to fill out your unique Coffeetheapp profile.

Who should use Coffeetheapp?

We have tried to make the app conductive to millennial users. That being said, if you are looking to grow your professional network in a casual, social manner then you should use Coffee! If you are a company trying to hire interns & entry-level employees then use Coffee! Finally, if you are a job seeker then you should definitely use Coffeetheapp.

Do I need a Facebook and Linkedin to use Coffeetheapp?

No. But, you must login using one or the other.

Coffee says it's trying to find more matches near me. What does that mean?

This means that you have swiped through the existent users that are around you. Your queue will be re-populated once we have loaded more matches onto your account.

Can I message someone that I haven't matched with?

No. You have to be matched to chat within the application.

I swiped left for someone, will I have another chance to see him/her?

For the time being, no.

Is Coffee available for Android?

No. Not right now.

I am a company interested in posting jobs on Coffee. How can I do that?

You can set up your account on the Coffee 'Employers' tab on the home page. This will allow you to set up a profile & post a job to our system.

When will the employer feature be available?

This will be available during the second week of July.
