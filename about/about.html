Our Story

About Coffee

Coffee started from a conversation on Tinder with a young investment banker. The banker expressed the challenges she encountered when trying to casually meet other talented young professionals in New York City. Her number one gripe was the inability to tap these networks to receive information on up & coming companies that were offering employment. Directly after this conversation Nathan, CEO & Sawyer, CCO began to build Coffee.

Our Mission

Coffee aims to humanize the job & internship search process by allowing young professionals to network with real startup founders, hiring managers & other entry level employees. Ultimately we hope to connect the next generation of professionals & alleviate the unemployment epidemic in the USA.
